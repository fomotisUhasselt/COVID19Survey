This table displays the amount of relational tensions between age groups.

Each box shows the proportion of the people in the age group on the vertical axis that report increased tensions with each age group on the horizontal axis. A darker red coloration indicates a higher proportion of participants reporting tensions.

E.g., on April 14, 16.95% of all participants that are over 70 years old, report increased tensions with persons that are 0 to 17 years old.

We have observed that especially children and young adults experience increased tensions with people from all other age groups. The opposite is shown as well: other age groups mostly report tensions with persons that are 0 to 17 years old.
