Bar charts describe the demographic backgrounds of the participants in the surveys.
  • participants: number of participants by survey
  • gender: participants grouped by gender
  • age: participants grouped by age
  • education: participants grouped by level of education
We notice that women and young adults are more likely to participate in the study. As a result these groups are better represented in the observed results .
The statistical models use demographical data from Belgium to correct the results for the overrepresentation of these groups.
