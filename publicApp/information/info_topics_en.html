At the end of each survey, participants were asked to share their opinions about the Corona Study. Approximately 20% of all participants used this opportunity to leave a message.

Based on a number of key words, we have pinpointed a set of recurring themes. Note that a participant could write about more than one of these themes.

For a number of themes, more than one key word was defined. It is therefore not recommended to compare the proportional occurrence of different themes; however, it is interesting to investigate the evolution of each theme through time. For example, we notice that during the survey of April 14, a larger proportion of participants mentioned education than during the preceding weeks.
